Regarding the poster competition at Research Conclave 2017

 

Poster Competition Guidelines

 

•   The posters and models will be visited by students, judges, and delegates.

•   Best posters & models/demonstration will be awarded by Springer.

Guidelines

 

General guidelines

  • The registration fee is ₹ 150/- per person.
  • There can be a maximum of 3 members in a group.

 

Accommodation will be provided on First Come First Serve basis.

 

Judging will be based on:

  • Research Problem
  • Execution with problem
  • Effective outcomes
  • Last date for abstract submission is 8th March 12 Noon.

 

Notification of acceptance : Will be sent by mail.

 

Poster guidelines

  • The size of your poster should be 4 (height) X 3 (width) feet.

 

Abstract submission guidelines

  • The maximum allowed size is 1 page, inclusive of references, tables and figures. The text should be formatted in A4 format (210 mm x 297 mm), with single spacing, font “Arial”, size “10” and page margins of “15 mm” on the left and right sides.  Download Template
  • While submitting the Abstract please name your file as Name_Dept (e.g. saurav_design).
  • Please submit your abstract at rcstudiitg@gmail.com
